1. 希区柯克变焦 (Dolly Zoom)
中文模板： "将焦段从24mm切换成{}mm的视角，使背景贴近主体，实现空间压缩感，保持主体景别不变"

核心术语： Dolly Zoom (或 Vertigo Effect / Hitchcock Zoom)

英文模板：

"Dolly zoom: camera zooms from 24mm to {focal_length}mm, creating spatial compression while keeping the subject's framing constant."

变量替换示例 (e.g., 70mm):

"Dolly zoom: camera zooms from 24mm to 70mm, creating spatial compression while keeping the subject's framing constant."

2. 上下左右平移 (Translation: Truck / Pedestal)
这是一个关键的区别：“平移” (píngyí) 在3D空间中指的是相机位置的物理移动 (Translation)，而不是镜头的旋转 (Rotation)。

中文模板： "镜头向{}平移{}分之一屏，保持俯仰不变、人物姿态不变"

核心术语：

左右平移: Truck (left/right)

上下平移: Pedestal (up/down)

变量映射 (Mapping):

{方向}:

"上" -> "pedestal up"

"下" -> "pedestal down"

"左" -> "truck left"

"右" -> "truck right"

{分数}:

"三" -> "one-third"

"五" -> "one-fifth"

英文模板：

"Camera {action} by {fraction} of the screen {dimension}, keeping orientation and subject pose constant."

注意: 我们需要明确是屏幕宽度还是高度。

左右 (Truck) -> width

上下 (Pedestal) -> height

变量替换示例:

中文: "镜头向左平移三分之一屏..."

英文: "Camera truck left by one-third of the screen width, keeping orientation and subject pose constant."

中文: "镜头向上平移五分之一屏..."

英文: "Camera pedestal up by one-fifth of the screen height, keeping orientation and subject pose constant."

3. 俯仰 (Rotation: Tilt)
中文模板： "将镜头拍摄视角变为{}拍，角度为{}度，保持镜头拍摄位置不变、人物姿态不变"

核心术语： Tilt (up/down)

变量映射 (Mapping):

{方向}:

"俯" -> "tilt down" (从高处看)

"仰" -> "tilt up" (从低处看)

{角度}: "15", "30"

英文模板：

"Camera {action} by {angle} degrees. Keep camera position and subject pose constant."

变量替换示例:

中文: "将镜头拍摄视角变为俯拍，角度为15度..."

英文: "Camera tilt down by 15 degrees. Keep camera position and subject pose constant."

中文: "将镜头拍摄视角变为仰拍，角度为30度..."

英文: "Camera tilt up by 30 degrees. Keep camera position and subject pose constant."

5. 人物姿态变化 (Subject Pose Change)
中文模板： "使画面中的人物{}，保持人物ID不变"

核心术语： 这是一个内容变化指令，相机应保持静止。

英文模板：

"The person {action}. Keep identity consistent. Static camera."

注：{action} 部分需要您根据 "人物{}" 的具体动作（例如 "挥手"、"跳跃"）自行翻译。

变量替换示例 (e.g., "挥手"):

中文: "使画面中的人物挥手，保持人物ID不变"

英文: "The person waves their hand. Keep identity consistent. Static camera."
